How it works

Balance and Overall score always count toward the quality tier.

For each dimension above:

5 = I love this — high scores boost the rating

3 = Neutral — this dimension is ignored

1 = I dislike this — high scores lower the rating

Example: if you set Acidity to 1 and a coffee scores 5/5 acidity, that hurts its quality tier. If you set Sweetness to 5, high sweetness boosts it.

Formula details

Each preference (1-5) maps to a weight from -1 to +1:

weight = (preference - 3) / 2

The quality score combines balance + overall (always counted) with weighted dimensions:

quality = (balance + 2×overall + w×sweetness + w×acidity + ...) / (3 + |w| + |w| + ...)

Dimensions set to 3 (neutral) have weight 0 and are excluded entirely. The denominator adjusts so the quality score always stays on the 1-5 scale.

This quality score determines the tier (Outstanding, Excellent, etc.) shown on your coffee cards, stats page, and insights rankings.
