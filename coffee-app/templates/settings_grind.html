What data to use

Controls which score the algorithms optimize and whether to filter shots by brew parameters.

"Overall" = your holistic rating. "Taste average" = mean of 5 dimensions. "Ratio accuracy" = find the grind that hits your target output weight (set per coffee). Or pick a single dimension like sweetness.

Algorithm

Directed Search

Uses the simple rule: output too high → grind finer, output too low → grind coarser. Learns how sensitive your grinder is (grams per step) from your shot history and calculates exactly how many steps to adjust.

Automatically used when optimizing for ratio accuracy. Works from your very first shot.

Uses: grind size, actual output, target output (set per coffee), shot history for sensitivity estimation.
